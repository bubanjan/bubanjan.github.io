Biography

Novak Bubanja graduated gymnasium in Budva, Montenegro. Graduated doctoral studies in arts on Academy for Art and Design in Belgrade. Graduated on specialistic studies of painting in class of professor Vladimir Velickovic,famous serbian painter at Academy of Fine Arts in Belgrade.

Graduated on Academy of Fine Arts in Belgrade, painting in class of profesor Gordan Nikolic While he was in gymnasium he was working for 5 years in artstudio of famous montenegrian painter Mihailo Jovicevic, Cetinje town, Montenegro.
